# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in our community is valued and respected, regardless of their identity or level of experience.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to creating a positive environment include:

- Being welcoming and inclusive to newcomers
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination based on any protected characteristic
- Insulting or derogatory comments
- Personal attacks or unwelcome sexual attention
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and investigated appropriately.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed by the project, including but not limited to GitHub issues, discussions, and any other community channels.
